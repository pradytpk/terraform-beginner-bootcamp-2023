How to Make Sushi

Sushi is a delicious Japanese dish made of vinegared rice combined with various ingredients such as seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruit. It is often served with pickled ginger, soy sauce, and wasabi.

Ingredients:

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Fresh fish or seafood
  • Vegetables (e.g., cucumber, avocado)
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled ginger

Steps to Make Sushi:

  1. Prepare the sushi rice according to package instructions.
  2. Place a bamboo sushi rolling mat on a clean surface and put a sheet of plastic wrap on top.
  3. Place a sheet of nori on the plastic wrap, shiny side down.
  4. Wet your hands to prevent the rice from sticking, and spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving a small border at the top.
  5. Add your choice of fillings (fish, vegetables) on top of the rice.
  6. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat, applying gentle pressure as you go.
  7. Use a sharp knife to slice the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces.
  8. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Enjoy your homemade sushi!
